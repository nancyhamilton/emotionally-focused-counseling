Nancy is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with over 25 years of experience helping couples just like you. Nancy has the education, experience and expertise in helping you find the clarity you need so that you can find your way forward.

       

Nancy’s practice specialty is couples counseling. Her signature program, “Emotionally Focused Couples Counseling” is for highly motivated couples that are struggling to get the love and passion back or that are teetering on the brink of divorce or somewhere in between.

       

Nancy uses groundbreaking techniques based in brain science and attachment psychology to help you:

             
  • identify the patterns that keep you stuck;
    •          
  • develop tools for communication…even when you disagree;
    •          
  • facilitate renewed trust, despite breaches such as infidelity;
    •          
  • deepen or re-establish emotional and physical intimacy;
    •          
  • create a roadmap for enjoying a more securely attached relationship that can weather any storm.
