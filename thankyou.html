Emotionally Focused Couples Counseling:

   

Thank you!

   

Now that you have chosen your appointment time and completed the application,Nancy will call you for your complimentary Breakthrough/Clarity call at the time you indicated. The call will last approximately 30 minutes. This will allow Nancy to get to know bit about you and what you’re experiencing in the relationship right now. Then she will create a game plan for the next steps to building the loving,secure and intimate relationship that you’ve been longing for.


   

If you cannot keep your appointment for ANY reason, please be courteous and let us know within 24 hours of your appointment time.
