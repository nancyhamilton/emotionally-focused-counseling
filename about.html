Meet Nancy Hamilton

 
             

Nancy Hamilton is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker in Louisville, Colorado. She was awarded her Bachelor of Arts in Humanities and Psychology from The University of Colorado, Boulder and her Master of Social Work from Smith College, Northampton, Massachusetts.  Nancy provides comprehensive therapy and executive coaching services to couples and families.  Nancy offers in-office sessions as well as phone, Skype and Facetime sessions for out-of-state couples or couples that prefer to receive services from the comfort of their home or office.

       

Author

Nancy has written articles for national magazines and is currently co-authoring a book, What Would My Therapist Say.

Public Speaker

Nancy has spoken at numerous events including her most recent presentation, “Managing Complex Change in Relationships” for the Women of Wellness Weekend held annually at the Broadmoor Hotel in Colorado Springs, Colorado.

TV/Radio

Nancy has been a guest on both radio and national television including The Dr. Phil Show, Dateline and Nightline.
